About
Us

Robox is a consortium of
four companies:
Langen Consulting Engeneering

For over 30 years, we have been working with customers to develop cost-effective, modern and ergonomic concepts for the automation of logistics and technological processes and are guided by them during the implementation.

The group of clients of LCE consists of small, medium-sized and large companies, from all sectors. Through a network of external partners established over many years, LCE can provide solutions for ancillary aspects of logistics alongside its own concepts for the in-house arrangements.

ADUK

A Germany-based electronics development company founded in 2004 with a mission – to make machines smart.

We’ve gone through the way from idea to production with our own innovative Smart Factory products compatible with highest international standards. At the same time, we advise and deliver HW/SW engineering services and complex end-to-end M2M solutions for our clients worldwide. By a combination of our HW/SW experiences with a data-driven approach, we gain unique expertise & knowledge in machine learning and artificial intelligence and got an edge on the IoT market.

Nordcom Global

We develop and maintain a wide range of information products, including but not limited to management information systems, highly loaded SaaS resources and mobile applications.

We offer to our clients: digital platforms for creating digital ecosystems, enterprise automation, decision support systems and solutions on information security.

Intellectual Technik System

We have formed a research center to study the subject of face recognition, objects and sound flow. Our projects in the field of work with sound are applied in the public and civil sector.

The technologies that we use have become in demand for working with video streams. On the basis of the R&D company, we conduct research on the use of computer vision in the various industries. A number of unique technologies have been patented within the company.
